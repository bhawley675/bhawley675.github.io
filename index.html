Welcome to Bass Yourself Music

Bass Yourself Music is a collection of music loving individuals who want to share their passion for music with anyone and everyone that has an interest in learning.

The music experience of this group comes from a background of musical know how learned through ensembles and many many hours of practice. Alongside that knowledge is an passion for sharing the love and knowledge of music with those who want to experience it for themselves. From that came the creation of Bass Yourself Music which as exanded well past simply offering bass lesssons.

If you have an interest in picking up a new instrument, there's no better time than now to start learning! Make your selection from a number of instruments offered, pick up the phone and schedule your first lesson at the premiere place for music learning!
